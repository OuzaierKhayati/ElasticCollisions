Elastic Collisions

Gravity: OFF

About: This is a simulation of Elastic Collisions where you can see how balls interact with each other.

Tips: Press, hold and drag the mouse to spawn a mass ball and control its velocity, be aware don't time travel if spawned many balls (it's heavy on the cpu).

  • Press G to toggle the Gravity ON/OFF
  • Press R to reset the simulation and empty the chamber.
